# Problem Statement: Simple Expense Tracker

## Problem
College students struggle with managing their daily expenses and maintaining budgets due to:
- Lack of proper tracking tools
- Complexity of existing financial apps
- No awareness of spending patterns

## Solution
A simple, user-friendly expense tracker that allows students to:
- Record daily expenses easily
- Categorize spending
- View analytics and insights
- Generate simple reports

## Target Users
- College students
- Beginners in personal finance management
- Non-technical users needing simple tracking

## Value Proposition
- Free and open-source
- No internet required
- Simple text-based interface
- Data privacy (local storage)
